# User Manual — E-Commerce Platform

This User Manual helps end-users understand how to browse products, manage their cart, place orders, track shipments, and manage their account within the E-Commerce Platform.

---

# 1. Getting Started

## 1.1 Create an Account

1. Go to the **Sign Up** page.
2. Enter your name, email, and password.
3. Click **Create Account**.
4. Verify your email if prompted.

## 1.2 Log In

1. Go to the **Login** page.
2. Enter your registered email and password.
3. Click **Log In**.
4. You will be redirected to the Dashboard.

---

# 2. Browsing Products

## 2.1 View All Products

* Click on **Products** from the top navigation.
* A list of items will appear with name, price, and stock status.

## 2.2 Filter & Search

* Use the **search bar** to find products by name.
* Apply filters such as:

  * Category
  * Price range
  * Availability (In Stock)

## 2.3 View Product Details

1. Click on any product.
2. View details including:

   * Description
   * Price
   * Stock
   * Ratings

---

# 3. Managing Your Cart

## 3.1 Add Items to Cart

1. Open a product page.
2. Select quantity.
3. Click **Add to Cart**.

## 3.2 View Cart

* Click the **Cart** icon in the top navigation.
* You can see all items, quantities, and total amount.

## 3.3 Update Quantity

1. Go to **Cart**.
2. Change the quantity using the + / – buttons.
3. Totals will update automatically.

## 3.4 Remove Items

1. Click the **Remove** icon on an item.
2. The item will be deleted from the cart.

---

# 4. Checkout & Orders

## 4.1 Add Shipping Address

1. During checkout, click **Add New Address**.
2. Enter your details.
3. Save the address.

## 4.2 Choose Payment Method

Supported payment methods:

* Credit/Debit Card
* UPI
* Netbanking
* Cash on Delivery (if available)

## 4.3 Place Order

1. Review your cart.
2. Select your shipping address.
3. Choose a payment method.
4. Click **Place Order**.
5. You will see an order confirmation screen.

---

# 5. Tracking Orders

## 5.1 View Order History

* Go to **My Orders** from your profile menu.
* All previous orders will be listed.

## 5.2 Track Delivery

1. Open an order.
2. You can view:

   * Current status (Processing/Shipped/Delivered)
   * Estimated delivery date
   * Tracking number

---

# 6. Managing Account

## 6.1 Edit Profile

1. Go to **Profile Settings**.
2. Update your name, email, or phone number.
3. Click **Save**.

## 6.2 Change Password

1. Navigate to **Security Settings**.
2. Enter old password.
3. Enter new password.
4. Click **Change Password**.

## 6.3 Manage Addresses

* Add, edit, or delete addresses from the **Address Book**.

---

# 7. Support

## 7.1 Contact Support

For help, you can reach support through:

* Help Center
* Live chat
* Email support

## 7.2 Common Issues

* Payment failed
* Order not updating
* Login problems

Refer to the Troubleshooting Guide for solutions.
